***Vim***


**Installation:**

        apt install vim

**Arrow Keys:**

        J: Down
        K: Up
        L: Left
        H: Right

**Jumping Keys:**

        6J: 6 Lines Down
        7K: 7 Lines Up
        15L: 15 Characters Left
        21H: 21 Characters Right

        w: next word
        e: end of next word
        b: before word

        0: first of line
        Shift + 6: first of line
        Shift + 4: end of line

        g: first of file
        num + gg: first of line num th
        G: end of file
